Dear Mr. Ochoa:

Please accept our warm greetings from the Corporación Autónoma Regional de La Guajira — Corpoguajira. We have received and reviewed your communication filed under number 2026-EE-RIO-003845, submitting the AQUA-8: Solar Desalination Plant for Wayuu Communities project, nominated for the RELX Environmental Challenge 2026.

After evaluating the technical proposal, the team's background, and the project's relevance to addressing the drinking water access issues in the municipality of Manaure and surrounding communities, we are pleased to inform you that Corpoguajira expresses its interest and willingness to provide institutional support for the development of the AQUA-8 project in the territory.

We believe this initiative aligns with the corporation's strategic objectives regarding water security, renewable energy, and community environmental management in La Guajira. Accordingly, we have designated the Community Participation and Management Office as the institutional liaison to coordinate the necessary actions with the Wayuu communities in the project's area of influence.

Facilitator Assignment

Mr. Jairo Manuel Epieyú Uriana, professional from the Community Participation and Management Office, is hereby assigned as the institutional facilitator for the AQUA-8 project. Mr. Epieyú will serve as the direct liaison between Corpoguajira, the project team, and the Wayuu communities, and will accompany all stages of socialization, consultation, and environmental permit management.

As a first step in this collaboration, we request your participation in a project socialization meeting with the Wayuu communities of the project's area of influence, to be held in the city of Riohacha with the following details:

Date:August 6, 2026
Time:9:00 a.m.
Venue:Corpoguajira Auditorium — Cra. 10 No. 15-55, Riohacha
Purpose:Socialization of the AQUA-8 project with Wayuu communities from Manaure, facilitators, and traditional authorities
Convened by:Corpoguajira — Community Participation and Management Office

Please confirm your attendance at your earliest convenience through Mr. Epieyú, whose contact details will be shared separately. This meeting is a fundamental step to ensure the free, prior, and informed consent of the communities and to establish the roadmap for the project's upcoming milestones.

Finally, we reaffirm our commitment to developing sustainable solutions for La Guajira's water security and hope this inter-institutional alliance will significantly contribute to the well-being of the Wayuu communities in the municipality of Manaure.

Sincerely,

Luz Marina Rojas Martínez
General Director
Corporación Autónoma Regional de La Guajira — Corpoguajira
CC: Community Participation and Management Office — Jairo Manuel Epieyú Uriana (Assigned Facilitator)
